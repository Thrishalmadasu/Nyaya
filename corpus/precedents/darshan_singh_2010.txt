                            J U D G M E N T
Exts.P2 and P4 are the applications made by the petitioner seeking

renewal of the permit in respect of Stage Carriage KL-7 D 4770 and for

substitution of the vehicle with a later model one bearing Regn.No. KL-

12/B.1735. The prayer sought in this writ petition is to direct the

respondent to pass orders on the applications expeditiously.
Taking into account the above grievance, the writ petition is

disposed of directing the respondent to place Ext.P2 before the RTA for

orders and pass orders on Ext.P4 also. This shall be done, as expeditiously

as possible.
