The    learned   counsel   for   the   respondents

                    submitted that this appeal is of the year 1983 and to

                    avoid further delay in disposal of the appeal the same

                    may be heard without the paper book. From perusal of

                    the record, it appears that the plaintiffs-appellants' suit

                    for specific performance has been dismissed by the

                    court below and the appeal is pending for last 27 years.

                    Therefore, to avoid further delay, I direct the office to

                    list this first appeal under the heading 'For hearing' in

                    the month of January 2011.
S.S.                                             (Mungeshwar Sahoo,J.)
